Five Nights at Freddy's 2

Five Nights at Freddy's 2 is a horror video game developed by Scott Cawthon. It is the second installment in the popular Five Nights at Freddy's series and serves as a prequel to the first game. The game is set in a fictional children's pizza restaurant called Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, where the player takes on the role of a security guard working the night shift. In this installment, players are tasked with surviving five nights at the restaurant while avoiding being attacked by the animatronic characters that roam the premises. The player must keep a close eye on the security cameras, doors, and vents to track the movements of the animatronics and prevent them from reaching the security office. The game features new and more aggressive animatronics, including Toy Freddy, Toy Bonnie, Toy Chica, Mangle, Balloon Boy, and The Puppet, each with their own unique behaviors and attack patterns. Five Nights at Freddy's 2 is known for its intense gameplay, jump scares, and eerie atmosphere, creating a sense of tension and suspense throughout the game. The game also introduces new game mechanics such as the flashlight and the music box, adding a layer of strategy and complexity to the gameplay. Overall, Five Nights at Freddy's 2 is a thrilling and terrifying horror game that will keep players on the edge of their seats as they try to survive each night and uncover the mysteries of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza.
